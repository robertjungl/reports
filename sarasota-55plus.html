💡 Janice's Cheat Sheet

🏆 Best Overall Value

Mira at Palmer Ranch — $1,695/mo gets you a gated, new-build apartment with in-unit W/D, granite, screened balcony. Hard to beat.

🎉 Most Social & Active

Solea Wellen Park — Weekly events, pickleball, bocce, pool parties, exercise classes, bar nights. All included. Residents rave about the community feel.

🏘️ Feels Like a Real Home

The Floridian Club — Single-story villas (not apartments). 921 sq ft 1BR. Gated resort community. Feels like owning a home without the hassle.

🍳 Most Included in Rent

Arietta — Daily breakfast + transportation services included. When you factor those in, the ~$2,078 is a better deal than it looks.

🧖 Best Amenities Per Dollar

The Pointe at Lakewood Ranch — $1,800/mo gets you a sauna, steam room, wellness center, beer tap room, library, and proximity to UTC mall.

🐕 Best for Dog Owners

The Alloro — Dedicated dog park. Also, Mira allows 2 pets up to 60 lbs.
