Is this legal?
Yes! JUNGL products are made with hemp-derived THC and comply with the 2018 Farm Bill. They're legal in most US states and available for shipping nationwide. Check your local laws for specific regulations.
How long do the effects last?
Drinks typically kick in within 15-30 minutes and last 2-3 hours. Gummies take 30-60 minutes to feel and can last 3-5 hours. Everyone's different — your weight, tolerance, and whether you've eaten can all affect timing.
What's the difference between THC and CBD?
THC creates the "buzz" — the euphoric, relaxing feeling. CBD is non-intoxicating and promotes calm without a high. Our drinks combine both for a balanced, smooth experience. The CBD helps take the edge off and prevents anxiety.
Will I have a hangover?
Nope! That's the whole point. JUNGL gives you the social buzz of alcohol without the next-day consequences. Zero calories of alcohol, zero hangover. Wake up feeling great.
